Basic Chemistry

Elements and Atoms

Elements are made of atoms. Atoms are the basic units of matter.

Examples:

  • Hydrogen (H)
  • Oxygen (O)

Compounds and Molecules

Compounds are made from two or more elements.

Examples:

  • Water (H₂O)
  • Carbon dioxide (CO₂)

Chemical Reactions

When substances change to make new substances.

Examples:

  • Burning: \( CH_4 + 2 O_2 \rightarrow CO_2 + 2 H_2O \)
  • Rusting: \( 4 Fe + 3 O_2 \rightarrow 2 Fe_2O_3 \)
