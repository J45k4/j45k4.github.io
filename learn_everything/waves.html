Waves

Types of Waves

Waves can be mechanical, electromagnetic, or matter waves.

Examples:

  • Mechanical waves: Sound, water waves
  • Electromagnetic waves: Light, radio waves

Wave Properties

This section describes features like wavelength, frequency, and amplitude.

Examples:

  • Wavelength (\( \lambda \))
  • Frequency (\( f \))
  • Amplitude

Wave Behaviors

Waves can reflect, refract, diffract and interfere with each other.

Examples:

  • Reflection
  • Refraction
  • Interference
