Differential Equations

Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs)

These equations contain one independent variable and its derivatives.

Examples:

  • First-Order Linear ODE
  • Second-Order Linear ODE

Partial Differential Equations (PDEs)

These equations contain partial derivatives and are often used in fields like physics and engineering.

Examples:

  • Heat Equation
  • Wave Equation

Numerical Methods

These are techniques for approximating solutions of differential equations.

Examples:

  • Euler's Method
  • Runge-Kutta Methods
