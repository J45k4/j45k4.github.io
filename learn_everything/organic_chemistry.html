Organic Chemistry

Alkanes

Alkanes are hydrocarbons with only single bonds.

Examples:

  • Methane
  • Ethane

Alkenes

Alkenes are hydrocarbons with at least one double bond.

Examples:

  • Ethene
  • Propene

Alkynes

Alkynes are hydrocarbons with at least one triple bond.

Examples:

  • Ethyne
  • Propyne

Aromatics

Aromatics are hydrocarbons that contain a ring of atoms with pi electrons delocalized.

Examples:

  • Benzene
  • Toluene
