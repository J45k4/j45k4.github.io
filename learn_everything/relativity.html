Relativity

Special Relativity

Deals with objects moving at constant speed, especially near the speed of light.

Examples:

  • Time dilation: \( t' = \frac{t}{\sqrt{1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2}}} \)
  • Length contraction

General Relativity

Extends special relativity to include accelerating objects and gravity.

Examples:

  • Gravity as a curvature of spacetime
  • Black holes
