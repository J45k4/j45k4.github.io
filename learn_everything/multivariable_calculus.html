Multivariable Calculus

Partial Derivatives

Partial derivatives are like regular derivatives but focus on changing one variable at a time.

Examples:

  • \( \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(x^2y) = 2xy \)
  • \( \frac{\partial}{\partial y}(x^2y) = x^2 \)

Multiple Integrals

Multiple integrals extend the idea of a single integral to multiple dimensions.

Examples:

  • Double integral of \( x^2y \) over a rectangle.
  • Triple integral in spherical coordinates.

Vector Calculus

Vector calculus deals with vector fields and the operators that act on them.

Examples:

  • Gradient: \( \nabla f \)
  • Divergence: \( \nabla \cdot \vec{F} \)
  • Curl: \( \nabla \times \vec{F} \)
