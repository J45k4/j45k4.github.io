Calculus

Derivatives

Derivatives help us understand how a function changes.

Examples:

  • The derivative of \( x^2 \) is \( 2x \).
  • The derivative of \( \sin(x) \) is \( \cos(x) \).

Integrals

Integrals are about finding the area under curves.

Examples:

  • The integral of \( x \) is \( \frac{x^2}{2} + C \).
  • The integral of \( e^x \) is \( e^x + C \).

Sequences & Series

These are sums of numbers or expressions in a specific order.

Examples:

  • Arithmetic Series: \( \frac{n}{2}(a_1 + a_n) \)
  • Geometric Series: \( \frac{a_1(1 - r^n)}{1 - r} \)
