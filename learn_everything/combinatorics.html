Permutations

Permutations are about arranging objects in a specific order.

Examples:

  • Ordered selection without repetition

Combinations

Combinations involve selecting objects where the order doesn't matter.

Examples:

  • Unordered selection without repetition
  • Unordered selection with repetition

Graph theory deals with networks of connected nodes and edges.

Examples:

  • Trees
  • Graph coloring
