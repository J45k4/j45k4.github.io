Electricity and Magnetism

Electric Forces and Fields

Describes how charges create electric fields and how they interact.

Examples:

  • Coulomb's Law: \( F = k \frac{{q_1 \times q_2}}{{r^2}} \)
  • Electric field (\( E \))

Magnetic Forces and Fields

Talks about magnets, and how moving charges create magnetic fields.

Examples:

  • Lorentz force
  • Magnetic field (\( B \))

Electromagnetic Induction

Describes how changing magnetic fields can induce electric currents.

Examples:

  • Faraday's Law
  • Lenz's Law
