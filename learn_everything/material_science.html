Materials Science

Metals

Metals are materials that are typically hard, shiny, and good conductors.

Examples:

  • Iron
  • Aluminum

Polymers

Polymers are large molecules made from smaller repeating units.

Examples:

  • Polyethylene
  • PVC

Ceramics

Ceramics are inorganic and non-metallic materials.

Examples:

  • Pottery
  • Porcelain

Composites

Composites are made from two or more different materials.

Examples:

  • Fiberglass
  • Carbon fiber composites
