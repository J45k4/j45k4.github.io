Thermodynamics

Laws of Thermodynamics

These are the basic rules that all thermodynamic systems follow.

Examples:

  • First Law: Energy cannot be created or destroyed
  • Second Law: Entropy tends to increase
  • Third Law: Absolute zero cannot be reached

Heat and Work

Describes how energy is transferred in thermodynamic systems.

Examples:

  • Heat \( Q \)
  • Work \( W \)

Thermodynamic Processes

Looks at how systems change from one state to another.

Examples:

  • Isobaric: Constant pressure
  • Isothermal: Constant temperature
  • Adiabatic: No heat transfer
