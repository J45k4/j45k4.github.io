Basic Arithmetic

Addition

Addition is the process of combining numbers to find their total. The symbol for addition is '+'.

Example: 5 + 3 = 8

Real-world Example: If you have 5 apples and get 3 more, you'll have 8 apples.

Subtraction

Subtraction is the process of taking one number away from another. The symbol for subtraction is '-'.

Example: 9 - 4 = 5

Real-world Example: If you have 9 candies and eat 4, you'll have 5 left.

Multiplication

Multiplication is the process of adding a number to itself a certain number of times. The symbol for multiplication is 'x' or '*'.

Example: 3 x 4 = 12

Real-world Example: If you have 3 boxes each containing 4 marbles, you'll have a total of 12 marbles.

Division

Division is the process of splitting a number into equal parts. The symbol for division is '/' or 'รท'.

Example: 8 / 2 = 4

Real-world Example: If you have 8 cookies and share them equally with a friend, each of you will get 4 cookies.
