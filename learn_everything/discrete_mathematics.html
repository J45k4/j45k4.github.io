Discrete Mathematics

Set Theory

Set theory is the study of sets, which are collections of elements.

Examples:

  • Union: \( A \cup B \)
  • Intersection: \( A \cap B \)

Graph Theory

Graph theory is the study of graphs, which model relationships between objects.

Examples:

  • Vertex and Edge
  • Directed and Undirected Graphs

Combinatorics

Combinatorics is about counting and arranging objects.

Examples:

  • Permutations
  • Combinations
