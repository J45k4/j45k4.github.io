Particle Physics

Elementary Particles

The most basic units of matter and energy.

Examples:

  • Quarks
  • Leptons (e.g., electron, neutrinos)

Fundamental Forces

The forces that act between particles.

Examples:

  • Strong nuclear force
  • Weak nuclear force
  • Electromagnetic force
  • Gravity

Particle Accelerators

Machines that speed up particles to study them.

Examples:

  • CERN's Large Hadron Collider (LHC)
  • Fermilab's Tevatron
