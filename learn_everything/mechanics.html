Mechanics

Newton's Laws

Newton's laws describe how forces work and how objects move.

Examples:

  • First law: An object at rest stays at rest, an object in motion stays in motion unless acted upon by a force.
  • Second law: \( F = ma \)
  • Third law: For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.

Kinematics

Kinematics deals with the motion of objects without considering the forces.

Examples:

  • Speed and velocity
  • Acceleration

Energy and Work

This topic focuses on the concepts of energy, work, and power.

Examples:

  • Potential energy
  • Kinetic energy
