Number Theory

Divisibility

Understanding how numbers divide is basic in number theory.

Examples:

  • GCD (Greatest Common Divisor)
  • LCM (Least Common Multiple)

Prime Numbers

Primes are numbers greater than 1 that have only two divisors: 1 and the number itself.

Examples:

  • Sieve of Eratosthenes
  • Fermat's Little Theorem

Modular Arithmetic

This involves doing arithmetic with remainders.

Examples:

  • Modulo Addition
  • Modulo Multiplication
